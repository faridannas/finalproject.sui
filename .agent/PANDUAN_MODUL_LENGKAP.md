## üé® Tips Formatting untuk Dokumen Final

Saat menyusun dokumen final (Word/PDF):

### 1. **Format Halaman**
```
- Ukuran: A4
- Margin: Top 3cm, Left 4cm, Right 3cm, Bottom 3cm
- Font: Times New Roman 12pt
- Line Spacing: 1.5
- Alignment: Justify
```

### 2. **Heading Styles**
```
- BAB (Heading 1): Bold, 14pt, Uppercase, Center
- Sub-bab (Heading 2): Bold, 12pt, Left
- Sub-sub-bab (Heading 3): Bold, 12pt, Left
```

### 3. **Code Blocks**
```
- Font: Courier New atau Consolas 10pt
- Background: Light gray
- Border: 1pt solid
```

### 4. **Screenshots**
```
- Center aligned
- Max width: 15cm
- Caption below image
- Border: Optional 1pt
```

### 5. **Tables**
```
- Border: 1pt solid
- Header: Bold, shaded background
- Center aligned
```
